ENIAC AND UNIVAC

John Mauchly and John Presper Eckert togerther built the first
successful electronic digital computer known as ENIAC(Electronic Numerical Integrator And Computer).
It was operational in 1946.ENIAC was 10 feet wide and 100 feet long; it occupied
1800 square feet of area and could perform 5000 additions per second.

In 1951, John Mauchly and John Presper Eckert developed another successful
computer,known as UNIVAC (UNIVersal Automatic Computer).It could handle both numeric
and textual information.It was the first commercial computer delivered to a business client US Census Bureau in 1951.
