Personal Computer

Computer has come a long way since then. in 1981,IMB
intoduced its first Personal Computer(PC) and in 1984,
they introduced the advanced version of the Personal Computer called
PC-AT(Advanced Technology).These computers were smaller in size,
faster in speed, more accurate, and more reliable.
